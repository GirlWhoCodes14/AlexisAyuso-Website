GaLexy Hub

iOS APP

SwiftUIXcode

Overview

GaLexy Hub is an online e-commerce iOS application for the sale of electronic goods.

The store sells various electronic devices, including phones, tablets, earbuds, and headphones, watches and televisions. Popular brands like Samsung, Apple and Sony are available on the platform.

Shop

Shop shows a rotating slide of promotional images at the top. It also shows the various electronic categories such as phones, tablets, watches, etc.

Search

Search the products in the shop by name or use filters to narrow down what you are looking for.

Products can be filtered by:

  • Device
  • Brand
  • Price Range

View Product Details

Displays the name, price, image, colors and product details. The product color is chosen by clicking on the thumbnail image of the color, which changes the image and the color name shown.

Cart

The image on the left is only shown when the cart is empty.

After viewing the product details, the product can be added to the cart. The default color will be used if the user does not select a different color.

The amount of items in the cart is displayed at the top. The cart shows the important product information. If the product has multiple colors, the specific color chosen is displayed. If multiple colors of the same product are added to the cart, they are shown as separate products.

The user can change the amount of a given product of a specific color (if applicable) by clicking the minus (-) or add (+) button. The total cost is tallied at the bottom of the cart.

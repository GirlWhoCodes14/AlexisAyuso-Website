iOS APP

Overview

GaLexy Hub is an online e-commerce iOS application for the sale of electronic goods.

The store sells various electronic devices, including phones, tablets, earbuds, and headphones, watches and televisions. Popular brands like Samsung, Apple and Sony are available on the platform.

Shop

In the search tab, the user can search for a specific device. You can also use the filter tool to search the device specification you are looking for.

View Product Details

When the user selects a product, they are directed to view the product details that typically includes colors, model number, and other device specifications. Of course, the name and price of each product is always shown.

The user can view the various colors. When you select a different color, the color name and product image changes. When you find the color of the device you are looking for, you can add that item into the cart.

Cart

The amount of each item in the cart can be increased or decreased, and an item can be deleted by clicking the 'trash' button.

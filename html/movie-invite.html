Movie Night Invitation

Simple iOS APP

SwiftUIXcode

Overview

The movie night invitation give details about the event and allows the user to accept or decline the invite. Then they can pick their favorite movie options for the movie night.

Movie Invitation

Pick Your Favorite Movies

When the user swipes left, they are voting against watching that movie. When the user swipes right, they are voting for watching the movie.
